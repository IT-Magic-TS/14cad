Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you'd like answered please free to email us.

How important is 3D Design?

Many times perfect 3D model designed by an experienced designer is the difference between a perfect product (profit) or total disasters. We repair few jobs started by others every year.

Metal or Concrete Stairs?

We design perfect 3D model of metal stairs. The same time we think about balustrade. We cut parts by water-jet or lasser CNC. Everything is precise and easy to assembly. Metal stairs are much better choice thane concrete one.

Why we use computer calculations?

Our business is 3D cad design. We built our web andandroid calculations to speed up design work. Time is money. Is possible to build simple balustrade just with calculations without any drawings.
